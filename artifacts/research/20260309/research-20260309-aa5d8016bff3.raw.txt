TradingView indicator: Liquidity candle by sebastien_menard. What it does This indicator automatically identifies the first 15-minute candle of the US market session (9:30–9:45 AM New York time) and evaluates whether it qualifies as a liquidity candle by comparing its range against a percentage of the Daily ATR (Average True Range). By default, the candle is considered liquid if its range exceeds 25% of the daily ATR.
