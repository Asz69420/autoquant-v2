TradingView indicator: mNAV.com Bitcoin Power Law Quantiles (time corrected) by martykendall. This indicator plots a Bitcoin power-law valuation model with quantile bands, using Jacobian weighting so that each day in Bitcoin’s history contributes equally to the regression. Traditional power-law models fitted on log-log charts tend to overweight early data. Jacobian weighting corrects this and produces a more balanced long-term trend estimate. The indicator shows where Bitcoin currently sits within its historical valuation distribution.
