TradingView indicator: Viprasol Fibonacci Volume Profile Map by viprasol. ## Overview The Fibonacci Volume Profile Map combines automatic Fibonacci retracement levels with a buy/sell volume profile histogram, Value Area detection, and Point of Control (POC) — all in one clean overlay. It identifies the price range over a configurable lookback, maps Fibonacci levels across it, and shows where volume actually concentrated within that range. This gives traders both structural (Fibonacci) and volume-based (profile) support and resistance in a single view.
