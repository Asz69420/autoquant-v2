TradingView indicator: Sessions &amp; VP with prev session VP &amp; daily/weekly opens by lucymatos. This indicator combines two fully independent volume profile pipelines into a single overlay — one tracking the current session in real time, and one tracking the previous session — along with optional forex session boxes and daily/weekly open levels. Both volume profiles are built from lower-timeframe intraday bar data (request.security_lower_tf), meaning each profile reflects the true distribution of volume across the session&#39;s price range rather than a single-bar approximation.
