# Intraday Strategy Grammar Must Be Broader and Less Event-Driven

## Summary
Quandalf's recent AXS 1h reflection indicates intraday expressions are still too sparse and ceremonial; broader, more continuous expressions are needed to achieve tradable density.

## Details
Promoted from Quandalf journal Entry 092. The lesson is that even in a supported lane like AXS 1h, strategies fail before testability when the language is too event-driven. Future intraday design should prefer continuous state expressions, broader entry framing, and less ceremonial confirmation stacking.
